Atlanta Asian Cafe/Restaurant Map

NQ Nguyen

This map uses Yelp API data to show Asian cafe's in the Atlanta, Georgia region. I used the Yelp API Crawler from the previous GEOG450 class and modified it to take the data I wanted. I chose this because I visit my family frequently and I thought it would be cool to map out a variety of cafes/restaurants to take my family to This map was created for my own personal use, however the concept of it applies to anyone interested in the region looking for cafes to visit. I color-coded the markers based on the average Yelp ratings received for each business and created an interactive slider that filters out businesses based on if they meet a certain rating threshold. I also added county boundaries to visualize where highly-rated cafes were in each county and added lines showing how far my family members live away from the family home. All the layers are toggleable and the markers show pop-up content about the cafes.
